# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 17, 2026**

ReadFirst does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All data, including your reading progress and app settings, is stored locally on your device.

## Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information. ReadFirst does not use analytics, tracking, or third-party services.

## Screen Time

ReadFirst uses Apple's Screen Time API (FamilyControls) to manage app access on your device. This data is handled entirely by Apple and is not accessible to us.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at https://github.com/vaslnk/ReadFirst/issues.
